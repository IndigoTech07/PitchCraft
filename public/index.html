How To Craft Winning Pitches

Follow these three steps to turn standard job posts into high-converting client proposals instantly.

01

Drop Job Details

Paste the client's explicit job title or target keywords into the input field. Providing specific niche markers (e.g., "Full-Stack React Node Developer" instead of just "Developer") helps our system align the entire presentation with the technical scope the client expects.

02

Inject Your Value

Quantify your background directly. Clearly outline your exact milestones, industry experience, and your Unique Selling Point (USP) such as a unique workflow, speed asset, or niche expertise. This gives the underlying AI engine the concrete evidence it needs to hook the client's attention.

03

Generate & Profit

Select your optimal pitch tone (e.g., Bold, Friendly) and matching target platform (Upwork, LinkedIn). Click generate to trigger a secure query that writes a highly optimized, character-capped 100–150 word proposal tailored to break past modern client screening pipelines.
